About Me

I live in Denver, CO, and am learning to code while working full time! With that alone, my schedule is pretty busy. When I have spare time, I love getting out to the mountains and hiking. I really value time with my family and close friends. Gameboard nights, nights in with a book, and watching old movies or Project Runway/reality romance shows are a favorite past time of mine. Put me in front of a puzzle or a project and I will dive into it for hours, so time to myself isn't too shabby. Once quarantine ends, I'm most excited for a patio beer after a hike! I also adopted the poster child for cat obesity, Harold, and he helps snuggle me through quarantine. Check me out on Insta!
